Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how CycleWorks ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your email address and password when signing up manually
  • Your email and name if you sign in with Google or Facebook
  • Optional profile details, such as phone number and user type (Homeowner, Buyer, Recycler)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and log you into the app
  • To personalize your experience
  • To secure your account

3. How We Protect Your Information

We store your information securely in our database. Passwords are encrypted and not accessible to anyone, including us. All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Authentication

If you sign in using Google or Facebook, we only collect the data you authorize, such as your email and name. We do not post to your social accounts or access your friends list.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as you have an active account. You can request deletion of your account at any time (see below).

6. How to Delete Your Data

You can request to delete your account and all associated data by emailing us at support@cycleworks.app or visiting our Delete Account page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, email us at support@cycleworks.app.

Thank you for using CycleWorks!
